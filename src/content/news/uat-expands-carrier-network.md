We're proud to announce a significant expansion of our carrier network across Eastern Canada, with new vetted partnerships covering Ontario, Quebec, New Brunswick, Nova Scotia, and Prince Edward Island.

This expansion directly addresses one of the most common requests we've heard from our clients: better coverage for regional routes across Eastern Canada, with the same standards of reliability and transparency you expect from UAT Solutions.

## What's New

**Expanded Quebec Coverage**

We've added dedicated carrier partners for high-volume lanes between Montreal, Quebec City, and cross-border routes to New England. French-language support is available for all Quebec-based carrier communications.

**Maritime Provinces**

Eastern Canada's Maritime provinces have historically been underserved by national freight brokers. We've built direct relationships with regional carriers who specialize in these lanes — meaning faster transit times and fewer relay handoffs.

**Ontario Regional Routes**

New partnerships strengthen our coverage for intra-Ontario lanes, including Northern Ontario routes that are often difficult to cover at competitive rates.

## Our Carrier Vetting Process

Every carrier we add to our network goes through a rigorous vetting process:

- **Safety rating review** — CVOR and FMCSA safety records checked
- **Insurance verification** — minimum coverage confirmed and on file
- **Reference checks** — we speak with shippers they've previously served
- **Trial loads** — new carriers complete supervised loads before full onboarding
- **Ongoing performance tracking** — on-time rates and claims history monitored continuously

This process is why our on-time delivery rate holds at 98% — and why our clients keep coming back.
